# Persistence / C2

## Objective
Retain access with acceptable operational noise.

## Enter when
You need re-entry or sustained tasking beyond the current session.

## Prioritize evidence
- host stability
- account durability
- task/service/logging surface
- beacon constraints

## Low-noise path first
Choose the least conspicuous persistence mechanism that meets the mission need.

## Typical skills/tools
- `persistence-and-c2`
- `red-team-opsec`

## Exit when
You have a justified, tested re-entry path or decide persistence is not worth the noise.
